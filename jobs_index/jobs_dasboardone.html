About Company

Sadhu was founded in 1992 to carry out operations, maintenance & repairs (O&M, MRO) on board ships, offshore structures and marine equipment. Today the group company Sadhu Shipping is owning and operating 25+ ships/ vessels and is listed on the Indian Stock Exchange.

Sadhu Offshore Engineering is in the business of construction, repairs & maintenance of marine assets including ships, offshore structures, and specialized marine equipment.

Sadhu Offshore has established itself as a leading ship repairer in India with ship repair bases in Paradip, Vizag, Pipavav, Goa/Karwar and Mumbai.

In Paradip, Sadhu operates captive integrated shipyard infrastructure comprising of 1no. graving dry dock, 2 no.s wet basins, a slipway, fully equipped marine workshops.

In Pipavav, Sadhu undertakes drydocking through an MoU with the new owners of the Pipavav shipyard.

Sadhu is in the process of setting up a floating dry dock facility at Mumbai Port to cater to ship repair requirements on the west coast of India.

It is the authorized OEM service representative of Kongsberg Maritime (erstwhile Rolls Royce Marine AS) and PAC holder. Sadhu provides services for waterjets fitted on ICG ships.

In vessel construction, Sadhu has worked with DPSU yards Mazagon Docks, Goa Shipyard and L&T Shipyard on the construction of hull and outfitting - engineering, electrical and weapon systems. It has built pilot launches, modern survey vessels, pollution control launches, and pontoons operating at major ports.

Sadhu is expanding into multiple geographies. It is a dynamic organization full of opportunities for skilled, motivated professionals.
