Drilling & Well Services Industry

The Drilling and Well Services industry depends on highly qualified and experienced professionals who are experts in their respective fields. However, the industry is also renowned for offering opportunities to individuals who are new to the field. With offices in a diverse range of countries across the globe and established business relations in many more, Atlas Professionals is able to tap into a broad and international pool of Drilling & Well Services professionals — ensuring that the right professional is introduced to the right career.

As per Zero Harm policies, Atlas Professionals ensures that all professionals meet industry-standard certifications such as BOSIET, HUET, or MIST, along with a valid medical certificate. Atlas also operates the Greenhand Programme — an industry-leading training initiative to prepare the next generation of professionals.
